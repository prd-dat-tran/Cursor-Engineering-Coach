The Observe section gives you a high-level view of your Cursor IDE activity.

- [Dashboard](/observe/dashboard/) -- Practice scores, skill finder, daily activity, and per-workspace breakdowns
- [Timeline](/observe/timeline/) -- Gantt chart of your Cursor sessions with request density visualization
- [Coding Moments](/observe/coding-moments/) -- Screenshot gallery from your Cursor sessions with story reels and workspace filtering
